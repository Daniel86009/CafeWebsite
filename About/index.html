We are a cafe based in Adelaide that focuses on a calming environment where people can read books and relax. Our expansive range of authors and titles offers an experience found nowhere else.

Our Rich History

The cafe was established in 2025 by a group of budding entrepreneurs who collaborated to create greatness. Our beloved chef Bob has shared our passion and experience and support us through the highs and lows of the company. We have also had a close relation with our local coffee bean farmers as seen above which are locally sourced from Western Africa. One of our key ideas would be supporting the local coffee bean business, now whenever you purchase from our cafe you are support the kind local farmers like James.
